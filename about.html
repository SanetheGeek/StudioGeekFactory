About Sane

Yo! My name is Tyrell Johnson, but my friends call me Sane the Geek. I am the creator of Studio Geek Factory, a small, one man team that delves into multiple avenues of Art and technology ranging from Illustration, Programming, Music, Web Development, Game Development, Web Comics, animation, and 3D Sculpting.

I've been making art since the early 2000s and have been refining my skills and style over the past 15+ years. There's still a lot I need to learn about, and I'm more than willing to learn so long as it's presented in a fun and educational manner. Currently, I haven't worked in the mainstream industry and I don't think I plan on doing so. I like having the freedom to do what I want.

Some of the projects that I've worked on are JIGGLYTOONZ, now defunct, JIGGLYWAIFUS, a better, more developed version of JIGGYTOONZ, which is on a long hiatus. SIMON, my web comic series that's been in development hell since 2020. SPICE & CO., an Adventure-Romance comic that I'm working on, Dr. Sane, a comedy based web comic I'm also working on. Gun Gluttons, my 1st 2D collectathon shoot-em-up platformer game, and countless 2D Illustrations.
